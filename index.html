Artemis Society Of Women

We, here at the Artemis Society of Women, aim to promote women empowerment and gender equality. We are a youth-led Non-Profit Organization trying to uplift gender barriers and amplify the voices of women. Through our endeavours, we hope to spread awareness of fundamental laws, menstrual equity, sex education, pay parity, women and children health, destigmatizing taboos, etc.
